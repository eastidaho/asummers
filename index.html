About

Born to be on stage

Annabelle Summers is a performer who brings the full picture to every stage — a voice that fills the room, movement that captivates, and the acting instincts to make a character live and breathe. Whether she's anchoring a musical theatre production, dancing a contemporary solo, or delivering a monologue, she commits completely.

She holds a Bachelor of Arts in Dance Choreography & Performance from Brigham Young University–Idaho (Class of 2022), with training spanning ballet, contemporary, jazz, and hip-hop. Her vocal and acting abilities round out an artistry that directors and choreographers love to work with.
